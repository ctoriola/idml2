Integrity DML Group Limited (IDML)

A sovereign-interfaced industrial catalyst dedicated to transforming Nigeria's automotive landscape through strategic partnerships, innovation, and localisation. We bridge world-class Chinese automotive engineering with the Nigerian market through our strategic framework agreement with Integrity Group (Hong Kong) Limited.

Our commitment is to establish a structured, sustainable, and bankable framework for the phased manufacture and local manufacturing of vehicles in Nigeria. We transition from simple importation to full-scale industrial deployment, fostering technology transfer and national economic growth.
