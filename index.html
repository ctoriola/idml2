Integrity DML Group Limited

Driving Nigeria's Automotive Revolution

Who We Are

Integrity DML Group Limited (IDML) is a sovereign-interfaced industrial catalyst dedicated to transforming Nigeria's automotive landscape through strategic partnership and innovation.

Our Mission

To establish a structured, sustainable, and bankable framework for the phased manufacture and local manufacturing of vehicles in Nigeria, transitioning from importation to full-scale industrial deployment and fostering technology transfer.

Strategic Partnership

Through our strategic framework agreement with Integrity Group (Hong Kong) Limited, we bridge the gap between world-class Chinese automotive engineering and the Nigerian market, enabling transformative industrial development.

Our Knock-Down Assembly Facility

State-of-the-art manufacturing hub empowering Nigeria's automotive independence

World-Class Infrastructure

Our Knock-Down (KD) Assembly Facility represents a milestone in Nigeria's automotive transformation. Equipped with cutting-edge technology and international quality standards, we assemble vehicles that meet global specifications while creating local employment opportunities.

Key Capabilities:

  • Complete vehicle assembly from imported components
  • Precision engineering and quality control
  • High-volume production capacity
  • SONCAP and international compliance
  • Technology transfer and workforce development
  • Environmental sustainability standards
